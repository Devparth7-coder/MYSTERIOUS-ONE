About Us

Founded in 2025 at Madan Mohan Malaviya University of Technology, MYSTERIOUS ONE was born from a shared passion for technology and innovation. Our club brings together students from all disciplines to explore the frontiers of modern technology.

We believe in learning by doing, which is why our members work on real-world projects, participate in hackathons, and collaborate with industry experts to solve meaningful problems.

Curiosity

We ask questions, challenge assumptions, and explore the unknown with an open mind.

Innovation

We push boundaries and develop creative solutions to complex problems.

Collaboration

We believe the best solutions come from diverse minds working together.

Founder’s Note
Welcome to MYSTERIOUS ONE — a club born out of curiosity and passion for decoding the unknown. Our mission is simple yet ambitious: to explore advanced technologies, create meaningful projects, and empower students who dare to think differently. Whether you're a tech enthusiast, innovator, or just beginning your journey — this is your space to grow, build, and discover. Let’s inspire one another and transform ideas into reality.

Keep questioning. Keep creating. That’s what makes us mysterious.
Dev Parth
Founder & President, MYSTERIOUS ONE
